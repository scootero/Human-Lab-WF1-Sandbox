# Features

## Feature 1

**Title:** Science-backed experiment catalog

Browse curated experiments with peer-reviewed sources—not influencer hype. Each study links to real research so you know why you're testing something.

## Feature 2

**Title:** Structured daily check-ins

Complete short check-ins during your experiment. Track adherence, mood, and outcomes without spreadsheets or guesswork.

## Feature 3

**Title:** Category-based lab

Choose what you want to improve—stress, sleep, energy, nutrition, focus, and more. Each category offers experiments sized for real life.

## Feature 4

**Title:** Your AI lab scientist

Get guidance, encouragement, and context as you run experiments. The mad-scientist mascot keeps the process playful while staying grounded in evidence.

## Feature 5

**Title:** Clear results

When your experiment ends, review what happened. See whether the intervention worked for you—not for an average user in a marketing slide.
