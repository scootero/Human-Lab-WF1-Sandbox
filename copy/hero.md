Human Lab turns self-improvement into structured, science-backed experiments. Pick a category—stress, sleep, energy, or habits—follow a guided study for 7 to 45 days, check in daily, and see whether the change made a real difference for you.

No miracle claims. No generic advice. Just your data, your experiments, and an AI scientist in your corner.
