# FAQ

## What is Human Lab?

Human Lab is a self-experimentation app for iOS. You pick a science-backed experiment, run it for a set number of days, check in daily, and review whether it actually improved your target outcome.

## How is this different from a habit tracker?

Habit trackers help you repeat behaviors. Human Lab helps you test whether a specific intervention works for you—with a start date, end date, daily data, and a results summary. Think n-of-1 experiment, not endless streaks.

## Do I need to be a scientist to use it?

No. Experiments are pre-designed with plain-language instructions and linked research. You follow the protocol, log check-ins, and review results when the study completes.

## How much does it cost?

Human Lab offers a 7-day free trial, then $6.99/month. Cancel anytime from your account settings. Pricing may change before public launch—see the landing page for current terms.

## Is my data private?

Your experiment data is yours. We do not sell personal health data. A full privacy policy will be published before launch.

## What platforms are supported?

The full product targets iOS. This package includes an interactive web mockup for validation—the landing page embeds the deployed mockup URL; it does not import mockup source code directly.
